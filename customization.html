How We Customize Automation for Your Farm

Every farm is different. Our process ensures the automation system is tailored to your specific land, crops, and requirements.

01
Site Assessment

Our engineers visit your farm to understand your irrigation setup, crop types, power availability, and specific challenges.

  • Farm layout mapping
  • Existing infrastructure review
  • Challenge identification
02
Device Selection

Based on the assessment, we recommend the right combination of devices — no unnecessary hardware, no missing components.

  • Need-based selection
  • Budget consideration
  • Scalability planning
03
Rule Configuration

We configure automation rules specific to your crops, soil type, and irrigation method. Every threshold is tuned to your conditions.

  • Crop-specific settings
  • Custom alert thresholds
  • Automation logic setup
04
Installation & Training

Professional on-site installation with hands-on training for you and your team on how to use and monitor the system.

  • Hardware installation
  • System testing
  • User training
05
Monitoring & Support

Ongoing system monitoring and responsive support. We help you adjust settings as conditions change through the season.

  • Remote monitoring
  • Seasonal adjustments
  • Technical support
