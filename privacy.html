Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE DATE: JULY 2, 2026

At AgriQion, we value the trust you place in us when sharing details about your farming operations, soil conditions, and water resources. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, secure, and process your personal and telemetry information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to configure, test, and maintain our custom farm automation devices:

  • Form Details: Your name, mobile phone number, email address, and farm address supplied when requesting a free site assessment or consulting with our engineers.
  • Agricultural Metrics: Land size (acreage), crop types cultivated, local soil textures, water source details, and current power grid infrastructure configurations.
  • Hardware Telemetry Data: Recorded statistics from installed AgriQion devices, including water flow rates (L/min), soil moisture values (%), motor voltage currents (V), sensor signal logs, and controller relay state histories.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal and agricultural data is utilized strictly to provide reliable service:

  • To customize the sub-GHz radio transmission thresholds and logic limits for your fields.
  • To contact you for local technical support and coordinate farm physical visits.
  • To analyze aggregated telemetry averages to refine pump control valve calibrations and water-saving algorithms.
🔒 Commercial Guarantee: AgriQion does not sell, rent, or lease your farm data or personal details to third-party marketing companies. All data gathered is used solely to coordinate and improve your farm's irrigation setup.

3. Hardware Security and Data Transmission

Telemetry signals sent via sub-GHz RF wireless link between our soil sensors, pump controllers, and the central gateways are processed through isolated industrial-grade local channels. In instances of remote network disruption, logging registers accumulate details locally to ensure operational safety.

4. Your Rights

You hold the right to ask for a copy of your collected telemetry data, request adjustments to your contact details, or coordinate the deletion of your historical logs from our database systems.
